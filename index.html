Things to check out before the trip

  • 👀➖Ticket and price from Osaka to Outlet, from Osaka to Kansai Airport. Klook offers JR pass 4 days $59.19
  • 👀➖Icoca card for subway and convenient store shopping
  • 👀➖Mobile phone plan, likely need two sims
  • 👀➖Print copies of your accommodation and ticket reservation
  • 👀➖Check the restrictions on carry-on and checked luggage
  • 👀➖Change your bills to autopay
  • 👀➖Notify your bank of your destinations
  • 👀➖Check what to fill for entrance form
  • 👀➖Buy tickets now for places you know you want to visit or see or make restaurant reservation
