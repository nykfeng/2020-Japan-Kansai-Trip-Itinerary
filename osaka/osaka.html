Shinsekai (新世界)

is a district in Osaka that was developed before the war and then neglected in the decades afterwards. At the district's center stands Tsutenkaku Tower, the nostalgia-evoking symbol of Shinsekai.

Tempozan Ferris Wheel

100 meters in diameter, this world-class ferris wheel will take you on a 15-minute tour of the sky. Its illumination is as beautiful as fireworks.

Dōtonbori (道頓堀)

Dotonbori is well known as an energetic arcade full of shops and eateries. It's really bright at night because of all the neon lights, which makes it feel like the city never sleeps.

Kuromon Ichiba Market

Kuromon Ichiba is a lively covered market which stretches for 580 meters. Nicknamed “Osaka's kitchen” because both local homeowners and restaurant chefs get their supplies here

Osaka Castle (大阪城)

Osaka Castle is a popular tourist spot and a striking historical landmark in the middle of a modern urban cityscape of high-rise concrete blocks.

Ohsho 餃子専門店 大阪王将

Established in 1969 as a gyoza specialist, Osaka Ohsho soon garnered a steady following of Japanese diners who grew to love its casual ambience and irresistible dumplings.

Umeda Sky Building

The Umeda Sky Building (梅田スカイビル) is a spectacular high rise building in the Kita district of Osaka, near Osaka and Umeda Stations. It is also known as the "New Umeda City".

大阪港 Port of Osaka

天保山摩天轮, 天保山購物中心逛街, 大阪海遊館 (可买套票全包) 建議中午可以來天保山購物中心購物用餐，這邊店鋪還算是有不少

通天閣区 Tsutenkaku

新世界商店街, 通天閣, 餐馆, Spa World 站在塔上能將整個大阪市風貌一覽無遺，而塔內也有筋肉人的展出, 3F 通天閣的博物館

梅田区 Umeda

梅田LUICA百貨, 梅田阪急百貨 (堂島蛋糕、Baton d'or、Grand Calbee), HEP FIVE摩天輪, 大阪生活今昔館, 天然溫泉浪速之湯, →梅田藍天大廈夜景

天王寺区 Tennoji-ku

大阪最高地標「阿倍野近鐵百貨（Abeno Harukas）」購物天堂；精品百貨 吃到飽平價美食、Uniqlo、H&M、Big Camera的平價商場「Q’s Mall」

道頓堀区 Dōtonbori

最多好吃美食、好逛服飾、好買藥妝店、電器店的所在區域，難波範圍非常大，道頓堀、心齋橋、戎橋筋、千日前、美國村、南船場都非常近，可以結合在一起逛

天満宮区 Temmangu

来到了大阪，热门的观光景点一定少不了这个拥有千年历史的大阪天满宫。地点位于天神桥筋二丁目附近，除了能参拜祈福之外，春季更是个赏梅的人气景点。
